Purposes

  • To improve the short-term ability and spatial ability of the player via a maze-like game.
  • To compare singing and smiling affect the player’s performance in the maze game.
  • Investigating singing and smiling before playing a maze-like game can reduce the mental workload on the player while playing the game.
